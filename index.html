Welcome


I am a postdoc in Jens Chluba's group in Manchester (UK). We study the spectral distortions of the Cosmic Microwave Background radiation.



I completed my PhD Thesis in July 2017, under the supervision of Aurélien Barrau in Grenoble (FR).
We studied the phenomenology of a singularity-free cosmological model derived from Loop Quantum Gravity.
