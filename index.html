Welcome

I am a postdoc in Colin Hill's group at Columbia University in New York. We develop tools for the analysis of data from the Atacama Cosmology Telescope and The Simons Observatory (both in Chile), and explore physics associated with the Sunyaev Zeldovich effect.
Between September 2017 and August 2020 I was a postdoc in Jens Chluba's group at JBCA in Manchester (UK). We studied spectral distortions of the Cosmic Microwave Background radiation.

 I completed my PhD Thesis in July 2017, under the supervision of Aurélien Barrau in Grenoble (FR).
We studied the phenomenology of a singularity-free cosmological model derived from Loop Quantum Gravity.
