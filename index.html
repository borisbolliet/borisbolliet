I am Lecturer at the University of Cambridge. I dedicate half of my time to teaching Machine Learning, Statistics, and Research Computing in the MPhil in Data Intensive Science. I dedicate the other half of my time to research in Cosmology, developing Machine Learning and Artifical Intelligence based tools for the analysis of cosmological data to test cosmological models. My favorite telescopes are the Atacama Cosmology Telescope and the The Simons Observatory. Both are in the Atacama desert in Chile. My favorite physics topic is the Sunyaev Zeldovich effect.
